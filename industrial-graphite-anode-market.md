<p><p>The industrial graphite anode market consists of three main types: graphite blocks, graphite plates, and graphite rods. Graphite blocks are large, solid pieces used in various industrial applications for their high thermal conductivity and resistance to high temperatures. Graphite plates are flat sheets commonly used in the manufacturing of fuel cells and electrodes. Graphite rods are cylindrical structures often utilized in the production of batteries and electrolysis processes. Each type of industrial graphite anode serves a specific purpose in various industries due to its unique properties.</p></p>
