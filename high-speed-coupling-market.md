<p><strong>The High-Speed Coupling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible Elastic Coupling</li><li>Torsionally-rigid Flexible Coupling</li><li>Rigid Coupling</li><li>Flange Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed coupling market includes various types such as flexible elastic coupling, torsionally-rigid flexible coupling, rigid coupling, and flange coupling. Flexible elastic coupling allows for angular misalignment and dampens vibrations. Torsionally-rigid flexible coupling provides high torsional stiffness while allowing for some misalignment. Rigid coupling does not allow for misalignment but provides maximum torsional rigidity. Flange coupling is used for connecting two shafts in a straight line. Each type of coupling offers specific benefits depending on the application requirements in high-speed machinery.</p></p>
