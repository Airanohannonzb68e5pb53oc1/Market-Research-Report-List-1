<p><p>Metal detectors for packets come in various types, including electromagnetic induction, X-ray, and microwave systems. Electromagnetic induction detectors work by creating an electromagnetic field to detect metal objects. X-ray detectors use high-energy radiation to penetrate materials and identify metal objects within packets. Microwave detectors emit microwave signals to detect metal objects based on changes in signal frequency.
