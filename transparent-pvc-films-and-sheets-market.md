<p><p>Transparent PVC Films and Sheets market offers various types based on the manufacturing technology used. Calendering Technology involves passing the PVC through a series of rollers to create thin sheets. Extrusion Technology involves melting the PVC and then extruding it into sheets or films. Laminating Technology involves combining multiple layers of PVC with adhesives to create a final product. Each technology offers unique advantages in terms of cost, durability, and versatility in applications for transparent PVC films and sheets.</p></p>
