<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal mass flow sensors are categorised into two main types: contact type and non-contact type. Contact type sensors come into direct contact with the fluid they are measuring, while non-contact type sensors do not physically touch the fluid. Contact type sensors are generally more accurate and precise, while non-contact type sensors are often used in applications where avoiding contamination or pressure drop is crucial. Both types of sensors are widely used in various industries for measuring and monitoring gas flow rates.</p></p>
