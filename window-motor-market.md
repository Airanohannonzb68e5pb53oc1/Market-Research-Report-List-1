<p><p>The window motor market can be divided into two main types based on the power source they use: AC (alternating current) and DC (direct current). AC window motors use electricity that periodically changes direction, making them suitable for high-power applications. On the other hand, DC window motors use electricity that flows in one direction, making them more energy-efficient and easier to control. Both types of motors have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
