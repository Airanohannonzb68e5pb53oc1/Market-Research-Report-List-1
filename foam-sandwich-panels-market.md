<p><p>Foam sandwich panels are a type of composite material made up of two layers of thin, stiff facing materials bonded to a lightweight core material. The core material is usually made of foam, such as polystyrene or polyurethane, which provides insulation and structural support. Foam sandwich panels are widely used in the construction industry for applications such as walls, roofs, and floors due to their high strength-to-weight ratio, insulation properties, and ease of installation.</p><p>The foam sandwich panels market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period.
