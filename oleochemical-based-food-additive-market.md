<p><ul><li>Anti-fungal Agents/Preservatives</li><li>Emulsifiers</li><li>Specialty Additives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oleochemical-based food additives are categorized into three main types: anti-fungal agents/preservatives, emulsifiers, and specialty additives. Anti-fungal agents and preservatives are used to extend the shelf life of food products by inhibiting the growth of mold and bacteria. Emulsifiers help stabilize mixtures of ingredients that would normally separate, such as oil and water. Specialty additives serve specific functions like improving texture, flavor, or appearance of food products. These additives are derived from natural sources such as plants or animal fats.</p></p>
