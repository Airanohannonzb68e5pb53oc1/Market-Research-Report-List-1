<p><ul><li>Single Core</li><li>Multi Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Silicone Cable market can be categorized into two main types: Single Core and Multi Core cables. Single Core cables consist of a single conductor insulated with silicone, suitable for applications that require a single electrical connection. On the other hand, Multi Core cables consist of multiple insulated conductors bundled together in a single cable, providing multiple connections in one cable. Both types are commonly used in automotive industry applications due to their flexibility, durability, and resistance to high temperatures.</p></p>
