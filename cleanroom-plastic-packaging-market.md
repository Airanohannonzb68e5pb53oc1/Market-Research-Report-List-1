<p><p>Cleanroom plastic packaging market categorizes into four types based on material: Polyethylene (PE), Polypropylene (PP), Polystyrene (PS), and Polyvinyl chloride (PVC). PE is known for its strength and flexibility, making it suitable for various applications. PP offers high chemical resistance and temperature stability. PS is lightweight and insulating, making it ideal for packaging sensitive electronic components. PVC is known for its durability and versatility, making it suitable for various cleanroom applications. Each material offers unique properties to cater to specific packaging needs in cleanroom environments.</p></p>
