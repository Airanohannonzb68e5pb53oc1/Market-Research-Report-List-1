<p><p>Tri tops, tri shorts, and tri suits are the three main types of clothing designed specifically for triathlon races. Tri tops are sleeveless shirts designed to be worn during the swim, bike, and run portions of the race, providing comfort and moisture-wicking properties. Tri shorts are padded cycling shorts that can be worn for all three disciplines. Tri suits are one-piece garments that combine the features of both tri tops and tri shorts, offering streamlined performance and convenience for triathletes.</p></p>
