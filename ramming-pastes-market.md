<p><p>Ramming pastes can be categorized into warm type and cold type markets based on their temperature requirements for application. Warm type ramming pastes are designed for high temperature applications, typically above 1500 degrees Celsius, and are commonly used in industries like steel making and non-ferrous metal production. On the other hand, cold type ramming pastes are used in lower temperature applications, below 1500 degrees Celsius, such as in foundries and glass manufacturing. Both types of ramming pastes play a crucial role in ensuring proper lining installation and maintenance in various industrial processes.</p></p>
