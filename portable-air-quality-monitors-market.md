<p><p>Portable air quality monitors come in two main types: Indoor and Outdoor. Indoor monitors are designed to track pollutants such as VOCs, CO2, and dust particles in confined spaces like homes, offices, and schools. Outdoor monitors, on the other hand, are used to measure pollutants like ozone, PM2.5, and NO2 in the ambient air. Both types of monitors help individuals and authorities to be aware of air quality levels and take necessary actions to protect their health and the environment.</p></p>
