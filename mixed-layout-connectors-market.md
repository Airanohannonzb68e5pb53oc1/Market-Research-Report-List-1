<p><ul><li>Pin Contact</li><li>Socket Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mixed Layout Connectors Market is segmented into Pin Contact and Socket Contact types. Pin Contact connectors have elongated, cylindrical pins that insert into corresponding sockets to establish an electrical connection. These connectors are commonly used in various electronic devices for data transfer and power supply. On the other hand, Socket Contact connectors have receptacles that receive pins for connection. They are widely used in automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications due to their durability and reliability in harsh environments.</p></p>
