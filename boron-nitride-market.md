<p><p>Boron Nitride market consists of three main types - Hexagonal Boron Nitride (HBN), Cubic Boron Nitride (CBN), and Wurtzite Boron Nitride (WBN). HBN is known for its excellent lubricating properties and thermal stability, making it ideal for applications in cosmetics, electronics, and ceramics. CBN is a superhard material used in cutting tools, grinding wheels, and abrasive coatings due to its high hardness and thermal conductivity. WBN, on the other hand, is a polymorph of BN with unique properties suitable for high-pressure applications and semiconductors.</p></p>
