<p><strong>The Readout Integrated Circuits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Readout Integrated Circuits</li><li>Analog Readout Integrated Circuits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Readout Integrated Circuits (ROICs) are critical components in imaging systems. Digital ROICs convert analog signals from sensors into digital data for processing and display. They are commonly used in applications like thermal imaging and digital cameras. Analog ROICs, on the other hand, process analog signals directly, making them suitable for applications requiring continuous data streams, such as spectroscopy and medical imaging. Both types play a vital role in converting raw sensor data into usable information for a variety of imaging applications.</p></p>
