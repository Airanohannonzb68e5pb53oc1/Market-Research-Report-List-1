<p><p>Braided composites are widely used in various industries due to their high strength-to-weight ratio. In the market, braided composites are available in different types such as biaxial, triaxial, and others. Biaxial composites have fibers aligned in two directions, providing strength and stiffness in those planes. Triaxial composites have fibers aligned in three directions, offering improved mechanical properties in multiple directions. Other types of composites may have fibers oriented in multiple directions or unique patterns to meet specific application requirements.</p></p>
