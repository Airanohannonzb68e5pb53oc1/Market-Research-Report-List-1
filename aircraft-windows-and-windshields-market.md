<p><p>Aircraft windows and windshields market can be categorized into two main types: cabin windows and windshields. Cabin windows are installed in the passenger and crew areas of the aircraft to allow natural light into the cabin and provide views to passengers. Windshields, on the other hand, are located in the cockpit area and serve as a transparent barrier to protect the pilots from wind, dust, and other elements while providing a clear view of the outside environment for safe navigation. Both types of windows play a crucial role in maintaining the safety and comfort of passengers and crew onboard aircraft.</p></p>
