<p><p>IO Relays are essential components in automation systems. There are two main types: Electromagnetic Relays and Solid State Relays. Electromagnetic relays use an electromagnet to mechanically switch the contacts, often used in industrial applications due to their reliability and durability. Solid State Relays, on the other hand, use semiconductor devices to control the switching of the contacts, offering faster response times and longer lifespans. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market, providing options for various applications.</p></p>
