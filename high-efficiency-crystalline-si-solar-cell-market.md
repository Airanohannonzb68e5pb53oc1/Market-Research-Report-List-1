BCSC features contacts buried under the silicon surface for reduced recombination losses, LFC uses laser to create contact points, HIT incorporates thin layers for efficiency, Back Contact cells have contacts on the backside for enhanced performance, Passivated Emitter cells have passivation layers for improved efficiency, while other types offer unique features for high efficiency solar power generation.</p></p>
