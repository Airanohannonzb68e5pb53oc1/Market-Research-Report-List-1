<p><p>Musculoskeletal diagnostic testing involves various types of imaging techniques such as X-Rays, Dual-Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DXA), Computed Tomography (CT), Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI), Ultrasonography, and other emerging technologies. X-Rays provide detailed images of bones, while DXA measures bone mineral density. CT scans offer detailed cross-sectional images, MRI scans provide detailed images of soft tissues, and Ultrasonography uses sound waves to create images. Other emerging technologies in the market include positron emission tomography (PET) scans, nuclear medicine imaging, and arthroscopy.</p></p>
