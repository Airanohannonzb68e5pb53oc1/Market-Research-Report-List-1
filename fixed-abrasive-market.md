<p><p>Fixed abrasive market types can be categorized into natural abrasives and synthetic abrasives. Natural abrasives are derived from naturally occurring materials such as sandstone, emery, and garnet, while synthetic abrasives are manufactured artificially using materials like silicon carbide and aluminum oxide. Natural abrasives are known for their hardness and durability, while synthetic abrasives are preferred for their uniformity and consistency in performance. Both types of abrasives play a critical role in various industries such as automotive, construction, and metalworking.</p></p>
