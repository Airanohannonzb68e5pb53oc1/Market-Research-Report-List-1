<p><p>Dog vaccines market offers a variety of vaccine types to help prevent diseases in dogs. Attenuated live vaccines contain weakened versions of pathogens to stimulate immune response. Conjugate vaccines combine antigens with carriers to enhance immunity. Inactivated vaccines use killed pathogens to trigger immune response. Subunit vaccines include only specific antigens to reduce side effects. Toxoid vaccines contain toxins that have been neutralized. DNA vaccines use genetic material to stimulate immune response. Recombinant vaccines involve genetic engineering to create antigens for immunity.</p></p>
