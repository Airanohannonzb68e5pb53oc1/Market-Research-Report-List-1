<p><strong>The Automotive Fuel Pump Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC Fuel Pump Motors</li><li>DC Fuel Pump Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive fuel pump motors market includes two main types: AC fuel pump motors and DC fuel pump motors. AC fuel pump motors are commonly used in vehicles that run on gasoline. They convert alternating current (AC) power into mechanical energy to pump fuel from the tank to the engine. On the other hand, DC fuel pump motors are typically found in diesel-powered vehicles. They convert direct current (DC) power into mechanical energy, fulfilling the same function as AC fuel pump motors but tailored for diesel engines.</p></p>
