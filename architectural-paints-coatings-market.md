<p><p>Architectural paints and coatings market can be categorized into different types based on their formulations, including water-borne, solvent-borne, and other market segments. Water-borne paints use water as a carrier for the pigments and resin, making them eco-friendly and easy to clean up. Solvent-borne paints use organic solvents as a carrier, offering better durability and adhesion. Other market segments may include specialty coatings like powder coatings, UV-cured coatings, and high solids coatings, each with unique properties and applications in the architectural industry.</p></p>
