<p><p>PVC Paste produced by the microsuspension method is available in two main market types: Homopolymeric PVC Paste and Co-polymeric PVC Paste. Homopolymeric PVC Paste is made from a single type of monomer, resulting in a uniform polymer structure with consistent properties. On the other hand, Co-polymeric PVC Paste is made from a mixture of different monomers, offering a wider range of properties and applications. Both types of PVC Paste are commonly used in various industries such as construction, automotive, and packaging.</p></p>
