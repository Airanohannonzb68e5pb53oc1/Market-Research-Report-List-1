<p><p>Water-based organoclay market is segmented into three main types: meal, powder, and superfine powder. Organoclay meal is a granular form that is easy to disperse in water-based systems. Organoclay powder has smaller particle size compared to meal, providing better rheological properties in water-based formulations. Superfine powder, on the other hand, has even smaller particle size, offering enhanced thixotropic behavior and stability. Each type caters to specific application needs in industries such as paints, coatings, and cosmetics.</p></p>
