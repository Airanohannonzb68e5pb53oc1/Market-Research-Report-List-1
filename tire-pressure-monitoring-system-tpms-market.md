<p><p>Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) is a technology that monitors the air pressure in a vehicle's tires. There are two types of TPMS in the market - Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. Direct TPMS uses sensors inside the tire to measure air pressure, while Indirect TPMS uses wheel speed sensors to detect underinflated tires. Direct TPMS provides real-time data and more accurate readings, while Indirect TPMS is less expensive and easier to install. Both types help improve fuel efficiency, safety, and tire longevity.</p></p>
