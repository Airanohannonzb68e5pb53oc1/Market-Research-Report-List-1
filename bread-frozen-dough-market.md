<p><p>There are four main types of bread frozen dough market: pre-fermented, pre-baked, unfermented, and fully-baked. Pre-fermented frozen dough is dough that has been partially fermented before being frozen. Pre-baked frozen dough is dough that has been fully baked and then frozen for convenience. Unfermented frozen dough is dough that has not gone through the fermentation process before being frozen. Fully-baked frozen dough is dough that has been fully baked and then frozen for resale or later use. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the baking industry.</p></p>
