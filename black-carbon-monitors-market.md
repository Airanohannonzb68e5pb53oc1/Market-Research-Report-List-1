<p><p>Black carbon monitors can be classified into two types based on their mobility and application: Stationary and Transportable. Stationary black carbon monitors are fixed in one location, typically at industrial sites, urban areas, or monitoring stations, to continuously measure black carbon concentrations in the air. Transportable black carbon monitors are designed to be easily moved from one location to another, making them ideal for temporary monitoring campaigns, mobile monitoring vehicles, or field research studies. Both types play a crucial role in tracking and understanding black carbon emissions and their impacts on air quality.</p></p>
