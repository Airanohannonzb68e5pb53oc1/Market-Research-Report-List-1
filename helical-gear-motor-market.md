<p><p>The Helical Gear Motor market can be divided into four main types: Parallel Shaft Helical Gear Motor, Right Angle Helical Gear Motor, Coaxial Helical Gear Motor, and Others. Parallel Shaft Helical Gear Motors are best suited for applications where the input and output shafts are parallel. Right Angle Helical Gear Motors are used when space constraints require the motor and gearbox to be at a right angle. Coaxial Helical Gear Motors have the input and output shafts on the same axis, making them ideal for compact installations. The Others category includes specialized gear motors for specific applications.</p></p>
