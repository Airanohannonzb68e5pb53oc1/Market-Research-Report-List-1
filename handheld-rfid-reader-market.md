<p><strong>The Handheld RFID Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency Handheld RFID readers</li><li>High Frequency Handheld RFID readers</li><li>Ultra High Frequency handheld RFID readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld RFID readers can be classified into three main types based on their operating frequencies. Low Frequency Handheld RFID readers operate at frequencies below 135 kHz and are suitable for short-range applications. High Frequency Handheld RFID readers operate at frequencies between 13.56 MHz and are commonly used for proximity cards and access control systems. Ultra High Frequency Handheld RFID readers operate at frequencies between 860 MHz and 960 MHz, offering longer read ranges and high-speed data transfer capabilities, making them suitable for inventory management and asset tracking applications.</p></p>
