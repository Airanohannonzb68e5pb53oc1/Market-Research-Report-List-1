<p><p>Semiconductor Dry Vacuum Pump market is segmented into different types based on their design and functionality. The main types include Cam, Claw, Screw, and Others. Cam pumps use rotating cams to generate vacuum pressure. Claw pumps use claw-shaped rotors to create a vacuum. Screw pumps involve rotating screws to generate vacuum pressure. Other types may include dual-stage, scroll, and rotary vane pumps. Each type has its advantages and is used in various semiconductor processing applications based on their efficiency and performance.</p></p>
