<p><ul><li>SMD Type</li><li>Plug-in Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multilayer High Frequency Inductors are available in two main market types: SMD Type and Plug-in Type. SMD Type inductors are surface mount devices that are soldered directly onto circuit boards, making them compact and suitable for automated assembly processes. Plug-in Type inductors, on the other hand, are designed to be mounted by inserting their lead wires into holes on a circuit board. These inductors are often preferred for applications where high power handling capability and easy replacement are required.</p></p>
