<p><p>Industrial hand gloves market can be segmented into two main types: reusable gloves and disposable gloves. Reusable gloves are typically made of durable materials such as rubber or leather and are designed to be washed and reused multiple times. They are a cost-effective and eco-friendly option for industries requiring long-term hand protection. On the other hand, disposable gloves are made of materials like latex or nitrile and are designed for single-use applications to prevent cross-contamination and maintain hygiene in industries such as healthcare and food processing.</p></p>
