<p><ul><li>LRA (Linear Resonance Actuator) Driver IC</li><li>ERM (Eccentric Rotating Mass) Driver IC</li><li>Piezo Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HD Haptic Driver IC market consists of three main types: LRA Driver IC, ERM Driver IC, and Piezo Driver IC. LRA Driver ICs are used for Linear Resonance Actuators, providing precise and high-fidelity haptic feedback. ERM Driver ICs are designed for Eccentric Rotating Mass motors, offering strong and robust vibrations. Piezo Driver ICs are tailored for Piezoelectric actuators, delivering quick and efficient tactile feedback. Each type of driver IC caters to different haptic technologies, providing diverse options for manufacturers in the haptic industry.</p></p>
