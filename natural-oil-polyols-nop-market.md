<p><p>Natural Oil Polyols (NOP) are derived from renewable vegetable oils such as Soy Oil, Castor Oil, Palm Oil, Canola Oil, and Sunflower Oil. These oils are used as feedstock for the production of polyols, which are essential ingredients in the manufacture of various polyurethane products. Soy oil has a high level of polyunsaturated fatty acids, while Castor oil is known for its high levels of ricinoleic acid. Palm oil is widely used due to its abundance and low cost, while Canola oil and Sunflower oil offer versatility and sustainability.</p></p>
