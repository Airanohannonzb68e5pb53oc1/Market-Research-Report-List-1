<p><ul><li>Tone Detector Subsea Cable Tracker</li><li>Pulse Induction Subsea Cable Tracker</li><li>Mag-gradiometer Subsea Cable Tracker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Subsea Cable Tracker market offers various types of trackers for locating underwater cables. Tone Detector trackers emit a tone signal through the cable for easy identification. Pulse Induction trackers use electromagnetic pulses to locate cables buried under the ocean floor. Mag-gradiometer trackers measure changes in magnetic fields to pinpoint the exact location of cables. Each type offers unique features and advantages for different underwater cable tracking needs, catering to various industries such as telecommunications, offshore energy, and marine research.</p></p>
