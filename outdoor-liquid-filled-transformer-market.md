<p><p>The outdoor liquid-filled transformer market includes two main types: Seed oil-filled and mineral oil-filled transformers. Seed oil-filled transformers use highly refined natural ester liquids derived from plant seeds, offering improved fire safety and environmental benefits. Mineral oil-filled transformers, on the other hand, use traditional mineral oil as the cooling and insulating liquid. While mineral oil-filled transformers are commonly used due to their cost-effectiveness and long-standing presence in the market, there is a growing trend towards the adoption of seed oil-filled transformers for their superior safety and environmental advantages.</p></p>
