<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous paper market can be classified into two main types: single-layer and multi-layer. Single-layer continuous paper is typically used for basic printing needs and is the most common type of continuous paper. On the other hand, multi-layer continuous paper consists of multiple layers that allow for duplicate or triplicate copies to be created simultaneously. This type of paper is commonly used for invoicing, receipts, and other documents that require duplicate copies for record-keeping purposes.</p></p>
