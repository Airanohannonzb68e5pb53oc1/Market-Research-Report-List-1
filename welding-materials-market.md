<p><p>The welding materials market consists of various types, including electrode & filler materials, fluxes & wires, and gases. Electrode & filler materials are used to create a weld joint, while fluxes & wires help prevent oxidation during the welding process. Gases, such as argon and helium, are commonly used to shield the weld area from atmospheric contamination. Each type of material plays a crucial role in the welding process, ensuring a strong and durable bond between the materials being joined.</p></p>
