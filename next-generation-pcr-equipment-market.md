<p><p>Next Generation PCR Equipment Market includes two types: Two-Step Next Generation PCR and One-Step Next Generation PCR. Two-Step Next Generation PCR involves two separate reactions for cDNA synthesis and PCR amplification, allowing for better control over each step. On the other hand, One-Step Next Generation PCR combines cDNA synthesis and PCR amplification into a single reaction, offering a simpler and faster process. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market, providing options for researchers and laboratories.</p></p>
