<p><p>Automatic Passenger Counters (APCs) have different types based on the technology used for counting passengers. The Infrared APCs use sensors to detect body heat and count the number of passengers passing through a specific area. Stereoscopic Vision APCs use cameras to create 3D images and count passengers based on their movement and shape. Other types of APCs utilize different technologies such as laser sensors or weight sensors for counting passengers. Each type of APC has its own advantages and limitations in accurately counting passengers in various transportation systems.</p></p>
