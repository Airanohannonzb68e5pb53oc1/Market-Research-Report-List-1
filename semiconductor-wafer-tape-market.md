<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Back Grinding Tape</li><li>Mold Release Tape</li><li>Dicing Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor wafer tape market includes three main types: back grinding tape, mold release tape, and dicing tape. Back grinding tape is used during the process of thinning the wafer to remove excess material. Mold release tape is used to protect the wafer during molding processes. Dicing tape is used during the dicing process to hold the wafer in place. Each type of tape plays a crucial role in the semiconductor manufacturing process, ensuring the precision and quality of the final product.</p></p>
