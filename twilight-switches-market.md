<p><p>Twilight switches are devices used to control lighting based on ambient light levels. They come in two main market types: DIN rail mounting and wall mounting. DIN rail mounting switches are designed to be installed on a standard DIN rail in an electrical panel, making them ideal for commercial and industrial applications. Wall mounting switches are installed directly on the wall surface, making them more suitable for residential and small-scale commercial use. Both types of switches offer convenience and energy savings by automatically adjusting lighting levels based on natural light.</p></p>
