<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The iToF Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>cwToF</li><li>gToF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>iToF sensor market types include cwToF (continuous wave time-of-flight) and gToF (gated time-of-flight) sensors. cwToF sensors emit a continuous waveform of light and measure the phase shift to determine distance. gToF sensors emit pulsed light signals and measure the time it takes for the signal to return to calculate distance. Both types of sensors are commonly used in applications such as robotics, automotive safety systems, and consumer electronics for accurate distance measurement and gesture recognition.</p></p>
