<p><p>Real-Time Locating Systems (RTLS) utilize various technologies to track the real-time location of objects or people. RFID technology uses radio frequencies to identify and track items, while Wi-Fi systems track location based on Wi-Fi signals. Ultra-Wideband (UWB) technology offers high precision tracking through short pulses of radio waves. GPS technology uses satellite signals to determine location. Each market type offers unique advantages and applications, catering to diverse industries such as healthcare, logistics, and retail for efficient asset tracking and management.</p></p>
