<p><p>Spiral wound reverse osmosis membrane market includes two main types - composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes consist of a thin film layer on top of a porous support layer, providing high rejection rates for various contaminants. Asymmetric membranes have varying pore sizes throughout the membrane, allowing for more precise separation of particles. Both types offer efficient water purification capabilities and are commonly used in industries such as water treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
