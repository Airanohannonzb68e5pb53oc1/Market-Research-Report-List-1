<p><p>Specialty Graphite Market Types include Isotropic Graphite, Extruded Graphite, Molded Graphite, and Others. Isotropic Graphite is a high-quality graphite with uniform properties in all directions, suitable for various high-temperature applications. Extruded Graphite is produced by extrusion and is used in industries like metallurgy and electronics. Molded Graphite is made by molding and is commonly used in applications like heating elements and crucibles. Other types of specialty graphite include fine grain graphite and synthetic graphite, which have specific properties for various industrial applications.</p></p>
