<p><p>Learning Management Systems (LMS) can be deployed in two ways: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based LMS is hosted on the internet, allowing users to access it from any device with an internet connection. This type of deployment provides flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premise LMS is installed on a company's own servers and managed internally. This type of deployment offers greater control over data security and customization options but requires more resources for maintenance and support.</p></p>
