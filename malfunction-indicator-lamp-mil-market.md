<p><p>Malfunction Indicator Lamp (MIL) market consists of two main types: Intermittent and Continuous. Intermittent MIL indicates sporadic issues that come and go, causing the warning light to turn on and off. This type of MIL may be harder to diagnose and may require ongoing monitoring. On the other hand, Continuous MIL indicates a persistent problem that triggers the warning light to stay constantly on. This type of MIL signifies a more serious and immediate issue that needs to be addressed promptly.</p></p>
