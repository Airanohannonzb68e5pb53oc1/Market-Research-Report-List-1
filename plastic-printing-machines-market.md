<p><p>Plastic printing machines are categorized into various types based on their printing technologies. Inkjet printing machines use droplets of ink to create images on plastic surfaces. Roto-gravure machines use engraved cylinders to transfer ink onto plastics. Letterpress machines use raised images to imprint ink onto surfaces. Thermal transfer machines use heat to transfer ink onto plastics. Other types of machines include flexographic and screen printing machines which are also commonly used for printing on plastic materials.</p></p>
