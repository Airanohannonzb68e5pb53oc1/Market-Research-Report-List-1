<p><strong>The Marine Hybrid Propulsions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diesel-electric</li><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Serial Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine hybrid propulsions have three main types: diesel-electric, parallel hybrid, and serial hybrid. Diesel-electric systems use diesel generators to power electric motors, providing flexibility and efficiency. Parallel hybrids combine diesel engines and electric motors to work together, offering power boost when needed. Serial hybrids use electric motors for propulsion while diesel engines generate power to charge batteries, allowing for reduced emissions and improved fuel efficiency. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in the marine industry.</p></p>
