<p><p>Electronics Access Control System (EACS) market types include Access Controller, Card Reader, Electric Control Lock, and Other. Access Controller systems regulate entry to a specific area by granting or denying access. Card Reader systems allow access through authentication of programmed cards. Electric Control Lock systems secure doors electronically by controlling the locking mechanism. Other systems may include biometric scanners, keypads, or intercoms for access control. Each type of system plays a crucial role in providing secure access control solutions for various premises and facilities.</p></p>
