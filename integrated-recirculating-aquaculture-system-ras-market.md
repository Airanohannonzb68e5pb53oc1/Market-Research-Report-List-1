<p><p>Integrated Recirculating Aquaculture System (RAS) is a sustainable method of fish farming that minimizes water usage and waste discharge. Closed Type RAS refers to systems where no water enters or leaves the facility, making it a completely enclosed system. Semi-closed Type RAS, on the other hand, allows for some water exchange, but still significantly reduces the environmental impact compared to traditional aquaculture methods. Both types of RAS are gaining popularity due to their efficiency and environmental benefits.</p></p>
