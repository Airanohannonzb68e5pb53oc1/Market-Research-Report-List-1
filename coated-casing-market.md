<p><p>Coated casing refers to different types of steel casings used in oil and gas wells that are coated with specific materials for protection against corrosion and other environmental factors. The four main types of coated casing are surface casing, intermediate casing, production casing, and drilling casing. Surface casing is placed near the surface of the well to prevent collapse, while intermediate casing reinforces the wellbore during drilling. Production casing is used to isolate production zones, and drilling casing provides structural support during drilling operations.</p></p>
