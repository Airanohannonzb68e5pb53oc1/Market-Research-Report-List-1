<p><p>Wall mounted power supplies come in various types such as single output, dual tracking output, dual isolated output, triple isolated output, and others. Single output supplies provide a single regulated voltage output. Dual tracking output supplies offer two outputs that track each other's voltage changes. Dual isolated output supplies have two separate isolated outputs. Triple isolated output supplies have three isolated outputs. Other types of supplies may include multiple outputs with different voltages or special features for specific applications.</p></p>
