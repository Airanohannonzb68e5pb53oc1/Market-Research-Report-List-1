<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Antifungal Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Azoles</li><li>Echinocandins</li><li>Polyenes</li><li>Allyamines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antifungal drugs market consists of various types such as azoles, echinocandins, polyenes, and allylamines. Azoles are the most commonly used antifungal drugs and work by inhibiting fungal cell membrane synthesis. Echinocandins target the fungal cell wall, disrupting its integrity. Polyenes bind to fungal cell membranes, causing leakage of cellular contents. Allylamines inhibit fungal enzyme pathways involved in cell wall synthesis. Each type of antifungal drug targets a specific aspect of fungal growth and is used to treat a variety of fungal infections.</p></p>
