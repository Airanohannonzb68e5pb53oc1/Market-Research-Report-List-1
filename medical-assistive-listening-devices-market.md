<p><p>Medical Assistive Listening Devices market encompasses various types of devices catering to different hearing needs. Ear-Based Hearing Aids are traditional devices worn in or behind the ear to amplify sound. Bone-Anchored Hearing Aids use bone conduction to transmit sound directly to the inner ear. Cochlear Implants are surgically implanted devices that stimulate the auditory nerve to provide a sense of sound to those with severe hearing loss.
