<p><strong>The Liquid Polysufide Polymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thiol End</li><li>Epoxy End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid polysulfide polymer market types can be categorized based on the type of functional groups present at the ends of the polymer chains. Thiol end liquid polysulfide polymers contain thiol (-SH) functional groups at the end of the polymer chains, offering excellent chemical resistance and adhesion properties. On the other hand, epoxy end liquid polysulfide polymers have epoxy (-O-CH2-CH2-O-) functional groups at the ends, providing better flexibility and toughness. Both types find applications in various industries such as aerospace, construction, and automotive for sealants, adhesives, and coatings.</p></p>
