<p><p>Oil-Based Printing Inks market includes two main types - Vegetable Oil-Based Printing Inks and Mineral Oil-Based Printing Inks. Vegetable oil-based printing inks are derived from natural sources such as soybean, linseed, and corn oils, making them eco-friendly and sustainable.
