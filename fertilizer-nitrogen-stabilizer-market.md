<p><strong>The Fertilizer Nitrogen Stabilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Urease Inhibitor</li><li>Nitrification Inhibitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fertilizer nitrogen stabilizers, such as urease inhibitors and nitrification inhibitors, are used to improve the efficiency of nitrogen fertilizers by reducing losses through volatilization or leaching. Urease inhibitors prevent the conversion of urea into ammonia, while nitrification inhibitors slow down the process of converting ammonium into nitrate. These stabilizers help to ensure that nitrogen remains available to plants for a longer period, increasing crop yield and reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
