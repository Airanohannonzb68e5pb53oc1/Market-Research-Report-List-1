<p><p>Alkyl phenolic resin is available in two main types: Lump and Flake. Lump Alkyl Phenolic Resin is sold in solid, irregularly shaped pieces, while Flake Alkyl Phenolic Resin is in flat, thin pieces resembling flakes. Both types are commonly used in industries such as adhesives, coatings, and rubber compounding. Lump resin is often preferred for its convenience in handling and storage, while Flake resin is chosen for its ease of dispersion and melting in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
