<p><p>Steering lock systems play a crucial role in preventing vehicle theft. There are three main types of steering lock systems in the market - T-Lock, Top Hook Lock, and Baseball Lock. T-Lock systems are designed to secure the steering wheel in place by locking it in a horizontal position. Top Hook Locks fasten the steering wheel at the top to prevent any movement. Baseball Locks secure the steering wheel by encasing it in a heavy-duty lock, acting as a physical deterrent for thieves.</p></p>
