<p><p>Airless sprayers are available in various types to cater to different needs. Pneumatic airless sprayers are powered by compressed air and are commonly used for industrial applications. Electric airless sprayers are powered by electricity and are suitable for both professional and DIY projects. Thermal airless sprayers use heat to atomize the coating material for a smooth finish. Other types of airless sprayers include hydraulic airless sprayers, which are powered by hydraulic fluid, and battery-powered airless sprayers for portability and convenience. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for specific usage scenarios.</p></p>
