<p><ul><li>Steel Shot</li><li>Steel Grit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel abrasives market can be divided into two main types: Steel shot and steel grit. Steel shot is spherical in shape and is commonly used in cleaning, peening, and shot peening applications due to its ability to produce a smooth and polished surface finish. On the other hand, steel grit is angular in shape and is primarily used for surface preparation and removal of tough coatings or contaminants. Both types of steel abrasives are widely used in industries such as automotive, construction, and metal fabrication.</p></p>
