<p><strong>The Aniline Printing Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>UV-Curable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aniline printing ink market comprises of three main types: Water-Based, Solvent-Based, and UV-Curable inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and have low VOC emissions. Solvent-based inks are durable and fast-drying, making them suitable for a wide range of substrates. UV-curable inks are cured instantly using UV light, leading to high-quality prints and reduced drying time. Each type of ink offers unique benefits and is used in various printing applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
