<p><p>Noble metal plating additives are commonly available in two main types: solid/powder form and solution/concentrate form. Solid/powder additives are typically in a compressed or granular state and can be easily mixed into the plating solution. On the other hand, solution/concentrate additives are pre-dissolved in a liquid form, making them ready to be added directly to the plating bath. Both types of additives serve the same purpose of enhancing the properties of the plated noble metals, but they differ in terms of their physical state and ease of use.</p></p>
