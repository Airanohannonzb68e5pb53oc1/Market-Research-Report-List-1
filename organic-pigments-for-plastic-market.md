<p><p>Organic pigments for plastic market can be classified into two main types: Azo pigments and Non-azo pigments. Azo pigments are synthetic organic compounds that contain a nitrogen-nitrogen double bond, which gives them vibrant and diverse color options. On the other hand, non-azo pigments are made from natural sources such as minerals, plants, and animals, making them more environmentally friendly. Both types of pigments are widely used in the plastics industry for coloring and enhancing the visual appeal of various plastic products.</p></p>
