<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide Fiber Market is categorized into Type I and Type II based on their properties and applications. Type I Polyimide Fiber offers high thermal stability, excellent electrical insulation, and superior mechanical strength making it suitable for aerospace, automotive, and electronics industries. On the other hand, Type II Polyimide Fiber provides improved chemical resistance, flexibility, and low dielectric constant making it ideal for applications in the medical, textile, and filtration sectors. Both types play a significant role in various specialized industries due to their unique properties.</p></p>
