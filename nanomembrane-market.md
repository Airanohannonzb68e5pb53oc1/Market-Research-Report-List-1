<p><p>Nanomembranes are thin films with nanoscale thickness used in various applications. The market for nanomembranes can be categorized into three types: polymeric membrane, hybrid membrane, and inorganic membrane. Polymeric membranes are made from organic materials and are flexible and cost-effective. Hybrid membranes combine different materials to enhance performance. Inorganic membranes are made from non-organic materials, such as ceramics, and offer high durability and chemical resistance. Each type of nanomembrane has its unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
