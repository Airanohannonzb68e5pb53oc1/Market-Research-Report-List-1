<p><p>Semi-permeable films are selectively permeable materials that allow specific molecules or ions to pass through while blocking others. Biomembranes are natural semi-permeable films found in living organisms, such as cell membranes. Synthetic membranes are man-made semi-permeable films used in various industrial applications, such as water purification and desalination. Both biomembranes and synthetic membranes play a crucial role in regulating the passage of substances and are essential for various biological and technological processes.</p></p>
