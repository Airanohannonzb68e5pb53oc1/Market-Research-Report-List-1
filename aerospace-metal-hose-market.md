<p><ul><li>Strip-wound Hoses</li><li>Corrugated Hoses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aerospace metal hose market consists of two main types: strip-wound hoses and corrugated hoses. Strip-wound hoses are made by wrapping a thin metal strip in a helical pattern, providing flexibility and durability for various aerospace applications. Corrugated hoses are constructed with a series of folds or ridges, allowing for flexibility and strength in high-pressure situations. Both types of hoses are crucial components in aerospace systems, providing reliable fluid conduction and containment for fuel, hydraulic fluids, and other substances in the aerospace industry.</p></p>
