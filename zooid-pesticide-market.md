<p><p>Zooid pesticide market types include animal toxin, insect hormone, pheromone, and others. Animal toxins are derived from venomous creatures such as spiders or scorpions, which are toxic to pests. Insect hormones disrupt the growth and development of insects, contributing to their control. Pheromones are chemicals released by insects to communicate with each other, which can be used to disrupt their mating behavior. Other types of zooid pesticides may include microbial agents or botanical extracts that have pesticidal properties.</p></p>
