<p><p>Food Grade Metal Coatings such as PTFE and FEP are widely used in the food industry due to their non-stick and corrosion-resistant properties. PTFE, also known as Teflon, is a popular choice for cookware and bakeware due to its excellent non-stick properties. FEP, on the other hand, is commonly used in food processing equipment due to its high resistance to chemicals and extreme temperatures. Other types of food grade metal coatings include epoxy and ceramic coatings, which also offer similar benefits in terms of non-stick and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
