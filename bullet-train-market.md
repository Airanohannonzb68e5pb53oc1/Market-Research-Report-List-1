<p><strong>The Bullet Train Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wheel on Rail</li><li>Maglev</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bullet trains can be categorized into two main market types: wheel on rail and maglev. Wheel on rail bullet trains operate by using wheels that run on traditional railroad tracks, allowing for high-speed travel. Maglev bullet trains, on the other hand, utilize magnetic levitation technology to suspend the train above the track, reducing friction and enabling even faster speeds. Both types of bullet trains are designed to provide efficient and rapid transport for passengers over long distances.</p></p>
