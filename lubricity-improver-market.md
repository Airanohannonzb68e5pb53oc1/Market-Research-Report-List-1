<p><p>The lubricity improver market includes two main types: acidic lubricity improver and non-acidic lubricity improver. Acidic lubricity improvers typically contain organic acids that help reduce friction between metal surfaces, improving the overall lubricity of the system. On the other hand, non-acidic lubricity improvers do not contain acids and rely on alternative chemical compounds to enhance lubricity. Both types of lubricity improvers play a crucial role in various industries where the reduction of friction is essential for maintaining smooth operation and preventing wear and tear on machinery.</p></p>
