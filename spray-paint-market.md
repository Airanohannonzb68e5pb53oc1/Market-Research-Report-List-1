<p><strong>The Spray Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray paint markets are typically categorized into two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based spray paints use chemicals like acetone or xylene to dissolve the paint and create a smooth finish. They are known for their durability and resistance to weather conditions. On the other hand, water-based spray paints use water as a base instead of chemicals, making them more environmentally friendly and easier to clean up. They are popular for indoor use and have lower toxicity levels compared to solvent-based sprays.</p></p>
