<p><p>The Ultrasonic Piezo Positioners market includes two main types: Linear and Rotary. Linear positioners move in a straight line, allowing for precise and accurate positioning of components. They are commonly used in applications where straight line movement is required, such as in manufacturing and research settings. Rotary positioners rotate around a central axis, enabling angular movement and precise positioning of objects. They are often used in applications such as scanning microscopy and semiconductor manufacturing. Both types of positioners provide high accuracy and resolution in positioning tasks.</p></p>
