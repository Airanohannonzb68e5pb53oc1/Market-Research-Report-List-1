<p><ul><li>Tapered-Edged</li><li>Square-Edged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sound insulation plasterboards are divided into two main types: Tapered-Edged and Square-Edged. Tapered-Edged plasterboards have tapered edges on the long sides, which allow for a smooth transition between boards and easier joint finishing. Square-Edged plasterboards have straight edges, making them ideal for achieving a flush finish when butted together. Both types of plasterboards are used in construction projects to provide sound insulation and improve acoustics within a space. Customers can choose between Tapered-Edged and Square-Edged plasterboards based on their specific project requirements and preferences.</p></p>
