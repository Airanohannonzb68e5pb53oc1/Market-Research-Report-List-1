<p><p>Linear Image Sensors and Area Image Sensors are two types of Organic CMOS Image Sensors used in various applications such as automotive, consumer electronics, medical imaging, and industrial. Linear Image Sensors capture one line of pixels at a time, making them ideal for scanning documents and barcode readers. Area Image Sensors capture an entire area of pixels simultaneously, allowing for faster and higher-resolution image capture, making them suitable for digital cameras and smartphones. Both types cater to different needs in the market based on their functionalities and specifications.</p></p>
