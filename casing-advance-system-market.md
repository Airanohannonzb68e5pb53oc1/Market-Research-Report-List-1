<p><p>The casing advance system market consists of two main types: Permanent casing and Retrievable casing. Permanent casing is installed in the borehole permanently and is typically used in scenarios where long-term structural integrity is required. Retrievable casing, on the other hand, can be removed from the borehole after its intended use. Both types of casing play a crucial role in maintaining well integrity and are utilized in various oil and gas drilling operations.</p></p>
