<p><p>Low Frequency metal detection systems operate at frequencies below 100 kHz and are typically used for larger metal objects and bulkier materials. These systems are ideal for applications where sensitivity to smaller metal contaminants is not as critical. On the other hand, High Frequency metal detection systems operate at frequencies above 100 kHz and are designed for detecting smaller metal contaminants in products. These systems offer higher sensitivity and are commonly used in industries where small metal contaminants need to be detected.</p></p>
