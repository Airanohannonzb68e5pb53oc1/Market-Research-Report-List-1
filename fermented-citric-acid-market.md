<p><p>Fermented Citric Acid Market involves two main types - Citric Acid Anhydrous (CAA) and Citric Acid Monohydrate (CAM). CAA is a white, crystalline powder used in food and beverage products as an acidulent and flavoring agent. CAM is the crystalline form of citric acid with one molecule of water, commonly used in pharmaceuticals and personal care products. Both types are produced through the fermentation of sugars by mold strains, resulting in a versatile and widely used ingredient in various industries.</p></p>
