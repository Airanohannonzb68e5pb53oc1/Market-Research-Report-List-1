<p><ul><li>Batch Type</li><li>Continuous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Galvanizing flux market can be categorized into two types: batch type and continuous type. Batch type involves the immersion of the metal into the molten zinc bath in small batches, making it suitable for smaller scale operations. On the other hand, continuous type involves a continuous process where the metal is passed through a series of rollers and coatings to ensure an even galvanized coating, making it more efficient for large-scale industrial applications. Both types cater to different scales of operations and offer various benefits depending on the specific needs of the users.</p></p>
