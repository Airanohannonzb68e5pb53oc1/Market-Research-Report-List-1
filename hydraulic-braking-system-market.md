<p><p>Hydraulic braking systems are of three main types in the market - Hydraulic Disc Brakes, Hydraulic Drum Brakes, and others. Hydraulic Disc Brakes use calipers to squeeze the brake pads against a disc to stop the vehicle. Hydraulic Drum Brakes work by using brake shoes to press against the inside of a drum to slow down the vehicle. The other types of hydraulic braking systems in the market include anti-lock braking systems (ABS) and electronic stability control (ESC) systems.</p></p>
