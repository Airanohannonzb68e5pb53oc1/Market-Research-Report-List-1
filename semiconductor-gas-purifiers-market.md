<p><ul><li>Point of Use Gas Purifiers</li><li>Bulk Gas Purifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor gas purifiers are used to remove impurities and contaminants from gases in semiconductor manufacturing processes. Point of use gas purifiers are small, portable devices that are installed directly at the point of gas usage, ensuring the purity of the gas being used. Bulk gas purifiers, on the other hand, are larger systems that purify gases in large quantities before they are distributed throughout a facility. Both types of gas purifiers play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and efficiency of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
