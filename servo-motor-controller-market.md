<p><p>Servo motor controllers can be categorized into two main types based on the number of phases: 2-phase type and 3-phase type. The 2-phase type controllers are simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for applications that require basic positioning control. On the other hand, 3-phase type controllers offer higher precision and smoother operation, making them ideal for applications that demand high performance and accuracy. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
