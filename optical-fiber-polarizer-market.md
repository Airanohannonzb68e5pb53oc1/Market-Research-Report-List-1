<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Polarizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PM-PM Fiber</li><li>SM-PM Fiber</li><li>M-SM Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical fiber polarizers are classified into three main types: PM-PM fiber, SM-PM fiber, and M-SM fiber. PM-PM fiber polarizers are designed to maintain polarization of light in both the input and output fibers. SM-PM fiber polarizers are capable of maintaining polarization in a single-mode input fiber. M-SM fiber polarizers are designed to receive light from a multimode input fiber and output polarized light in a single-mode fiber. Each of these types cater to specific requirements in optical fiber communication systems.</p></p>
