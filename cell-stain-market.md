<p><strong>The Cell Stain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acidic Cell Stain</li><li>Basic Cell Stain</li><li>Neutral Cell Stain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cell stains are categorized into three main types based on their chemical properties: acidic, basic, and neutral cell stains. Acidic cell stains bind to basic components in the cell and appear as dark colors under a microscope. Basic cell stains bind to acidic components in the cell and show up as light colors. Neutral cell stains work best on cells with neither acidic nor basic properties. Each type of cell stain is used for different cellular components and structures in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
