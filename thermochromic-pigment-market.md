<p><strong>The Thermochromic Pigment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reversible Thermochromic Pigment</li><li>Irreversible Thermochromic Pigment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermochromic pigment market is divided into two types: Reversible and Irreversible. Reversible thermochromic pigment changes color temporarily when exposed to heat and returns to its original color when cooled. It is commonly used in temperature-sensitive applications like mood rings and thermometers. On the other hand, irreversible thermochromic pigment changes color permanently when heated and does not revert back to its original color. It is often used in security applications to indicate tampering or overheating in electronic devices.</p></p>
