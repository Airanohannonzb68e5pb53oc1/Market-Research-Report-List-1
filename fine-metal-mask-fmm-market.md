<p><ul><li>Etching</li><li>Electroforming</li><li>Multi Material Composite Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fine Metal Mask (FMM) market consists of three main types: etching, electroforming, and multi material composite method. Etching involves using a chemical solution to selectively remove metal to create the desired pattern. Electroforming uses electrodeposition to build up metal layers on a substrate. Multi material composite method combines different materials to achieve specific properties in the mask. Each market type offers unique advantages and applications in industries such as electronics, semiconductors, and microfabrication.</p></p>
