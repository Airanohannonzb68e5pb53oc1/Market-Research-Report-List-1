<p><p>Master Cylinder Actuation Market is categorized into three main types: Hydraulic Type, Air Type, and Mechanical Type. The Hydraulic Type utilizes hydraulic fluid to transmit force from the pedal to the brake cylinders. The Air Type uses compressed air to generate force for braking application. The Mechanical Type employs physical linkages to transfer force from the pedal to the brake cylinders. Each type has its unique advantages and applications, catering to different needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
