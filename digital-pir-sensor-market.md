<p><ul><li>Single Element</li><li>Dual Element</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital PIR Sensor market can be categorized into Single Element, Dual Element, and Others. Single Element sensors use a single pyroelectric element to detect infrared radiation emitted by objects. Dual Element sensors use two elements to improve performance and reduce false alarms. Other types may include advanced features like pet immunity or adjustable sensitivity levels. Each type of sensor offers different levels of accuracy, range, and functionality to meet various needs in areas such as security systems, smart lighting, and occupancy detection.</p></p>
