<p><strong>The Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) Waxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-oxidized EVA waxes</li><li>Oxidized EVA wax</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) waxes are commonly divided into two main types - Non-oxidized EVA waxes and Oxidized EVA waxes. Non-oxidized EVA waxes are produced by polymerizing ethylene and vinyl acetate without the addition of any oxidizing agents. These waxes are known for their flexibility, low melting point, and good adhesion properties. On the other hand, Oxidized EVA waxes are modified with oxidizing agents to enhance properties like hardness, viscosity, and thermal stability. Both types of EVA waxes find applications in various industries such as adhesives, coatings, and plastics.</p></p>
