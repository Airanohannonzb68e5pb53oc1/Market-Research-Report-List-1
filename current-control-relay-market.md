<p><p>Current control relays are used in various applications for controlling and monitoring electrical currents. They are available in different types such as DIN rail mounting and panel-mount. DIN rail mounting relays are installed on a standardized mounting rail inside an enclosure, making them ideal for industrial applications. Panel-mount relays, on the other hand, are directly mounted on a panel or surface, often used in control panels and electrical cabinets. Both types offer efficient current control solutions for different applications.</p></p>
