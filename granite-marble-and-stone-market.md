<p><p>Granite, marble, and stone are natural materials that are widely used in construction and interior design due to their durability, elegance, and versatility. Stone is a broad category that includes various types of natural stones like sandstone, limestone, and travertine.</p><p>The market for granite, marble, and stone is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period.
