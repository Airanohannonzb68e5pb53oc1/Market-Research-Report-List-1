<p><p>PVC fittings are commonly categorized into PVC Schedule 40, PVC Schedule 80, and Others. PVC Schedule 40 fittings are lightweight and used for low-pressure applications, while PVC Schedule 80 fittings are heavier duty and suitable for high-pressure applications. The Others market includes specialty fittings such as CPVC, PVC DWV, and PVC pressure fittings. Each type of PVC fitting serves a specific purpose and is chosen based on factors like pressure rating, temperature resistance, and chemical compatibility.</p></p>
