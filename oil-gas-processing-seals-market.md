<p><p>Oil & Gas Processing Seals come in two main types: single and double seals. Single seals are used when the process fluid and environment can be contained within one seal. Double seals, on the other hand, have an additional barrier to prevent leakage in case the primary seal fails. Both types of seals are crucial in maintaining the integrity of oil and gas processing equipment, ensuring safety and efficiency in the industry.</p></p>
