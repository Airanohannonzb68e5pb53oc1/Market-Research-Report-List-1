<p><p>Chronic Idiopathic Constipation (CIC) treatment market offers various types of diagnostic tests including blood tests, sigmoidoscopy, colonoscopy, balloon expulsion test, anorectal manometry, colonic transit study, defecography, and others. Blood tests are performed to rule out underlying medical conditions, while sigmoidoscopy and colonoscopy allow for visual examination of the colon. The balloon expulsion test evaluates rectal sensation and coordination, while anorectal manometry measures muscle function. Colonic transit study assesses bowel movement patterns, and defecography provides detailed images of the rectum and anus during bowel movements. Other tests may be utilized based on individual patient needs.</p></p>
