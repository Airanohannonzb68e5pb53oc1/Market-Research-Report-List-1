<p><ul><li>Single Coupled</li><li>Double Coupled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engine-driven railcar movers come in two main types: single coupled and double coupled. Single coupled movers are designed to move individual railcars at a time, making them ideal for smaller operations or situations where precision is key. On the other hand, double coupled movers have the capability to move multiple railcars simultaneously, increasing efficiency and reducing overall operating costs. Both types of railcar movers play a crucial role in the transportation industry, providing businesses with the flexibility and reliability needed to keep operations running smoothly.</p></p>
