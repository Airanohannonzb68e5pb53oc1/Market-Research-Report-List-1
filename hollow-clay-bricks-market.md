<p><ul><li>Vertically Perforated Clay Bricks</li><li>Horizontally Perforated Clay Bricks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hollow Clay Bricks come in two main market types based on their perforation patterns: Vertically Perforated Clay Bricks and Horizontally Perforated Clay Bricks. Vertically perforated bricks have holes running up and down the length of the brick, allowing for better ventilation and drainage. Horizontally perforated bricks have holes running through the width of the brick, providing improved thermal insulation and reduced weight. Both types offer unique benefits and are used in various construction applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
