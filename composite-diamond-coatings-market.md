<p><p>Composite diamond coatings are used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and medical for their unique properties and benefits. The two primary types of composite diamond coatings are Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD). CVD coatings are formed through a chemical reaction at high temperatures, resulting in a durable and high-quality diamond coating. PVD coatings, on the other hand, are created through a physical process using a vacuum chamber, offering good adhesion and wear resistance. Both types of coatings have specific applications and advantages in the market.</p></p>
