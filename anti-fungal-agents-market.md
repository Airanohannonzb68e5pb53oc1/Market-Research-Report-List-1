<p><strong>The Anti-Fungal Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Azoles</li><li>Polyenes</li><li>5-fluorocytosine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-fungal agents are classified into three main types in the market: Azoles, Polyenes, and 5-fluorocytosine. Azoles work by inhibiting the synthesis of ergosterol, an essential component of fungal cell membranes. Polyenes disrupt the fungal cell membrane by binding to ergosterol. 5-fluorocytosine interferes with the synthesis of fungal DNA and RNA. Each type of anti-fungal agent targets a different aspect of the fungal cell's structure or function, providing a range of options for treating fungal infections.</p></p>
