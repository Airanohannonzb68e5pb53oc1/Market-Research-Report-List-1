<p><ul><li>Ceramic capacitor</li><li>Film/paper capacitors</li><li>Aluminum capacitors</li><li>Tantalum/niobium capacitors</li><li>Double-layer/super capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical capacitors are categorized into various types based on their materials and applications. Ceramic capacitors are widely used for their stability and reliability. Film/paper capacitors offer high capacitance values and low losses. Aluminum capacitors are known for their high voltage tolerance and long lifespan. Tantalum/niobium capacitors are compact and have high capacitance for their size. Double-layer/super capacitors provide high power density and are ideal for quick energy storage and discharge in various electronic devices.</p></p>
