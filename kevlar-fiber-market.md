<p><ul><li>Para Aramid –PPTA (1414)</li><li>Meta Aramid –MPIA (1313)</li><li>Other Aramid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kevlar Fiber Market Types include Para-Aramid (PPTA 1414) and Meta-Aramid (MPIA 1313). Para-Aramid fibers, such as PPTA, offer high strength and resistance to heat, chemicals, and abrasion, making them ideal for applications like bulletproof vests and high-performance tires. Meta-Aramid fibers, like MPIA, provide flame resistance and thermal stability, suited for uses in protective clothing and firefighting gear. Other Aramid fibers in the market offer similar properties and find applications in diverse industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
