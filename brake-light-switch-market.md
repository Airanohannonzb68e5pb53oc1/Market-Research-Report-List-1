<p><strong>The Brake Light Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front</li><li>Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake light switch market is divided into two main types: front and side. Front brake light switches are usually located near the brake pedal, making them easily accessible for the driver to activate when needed. Side brake light switches, on the other hand, are usually positioned on the side of the vehicle and are activated by pressure from the brake system. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of vehicles by signaling to other drivers when the brakes are being applied.</p></p>
