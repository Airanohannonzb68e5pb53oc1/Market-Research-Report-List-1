<p><p>Transparent acrylic sheets are widely used for various applications due to their durability and versatility. There are two main types of acrylic sheets in the market: Extruded Acrylic Sheet and Cast Acrylic Sheet. Extruded acrylic sheets are manufactured using a continuous process, resulting in a more affordable option with lower optical quality. On the other hand, cast acrylic sheets are produced by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, yielding higher quality sheets with better transparency and surface finish, making them suitable for premium applications.</p></p>
