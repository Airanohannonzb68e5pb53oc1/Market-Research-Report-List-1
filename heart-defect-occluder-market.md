<p><p>The heart defect occluder market includes devices used to treat three main types of defects: Patent Duct (PDA) occluders for closing abnormal connections between two major blood vessels near the heart, Atrial Septal Defect (ASD) occluders for closing holes in the wall between the heart's upper chambers, and Ventricular Septal Defect (VSD) occluders for closing holes in the wall between the heart's lower chambers. These devices are crucial in minimally invasive procedures to correct these congenital heart defects.</p></p>
