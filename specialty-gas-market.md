<p><p>The Specialty Gas market can be categorized into various types based on their unique properties and applications. High Purity Gases are gases with extremely low levels of impurities, commonly used in sensitive industries like electronics and healthcare. Noble Gases are inert gases, like helium and argon, used in lighting, welding, and analytical applications. Carbon Gases include carbon dioxide and methane, used in food processing and environmental monitoring. Halogen Gases, such as fluorine and chlorine, are used in manufacturing and water treatment. Other specialty gases include rare gases, ammonia, and hydrogen.</p></p>
