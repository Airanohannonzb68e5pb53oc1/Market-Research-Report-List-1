<p><p>Photovoltaic installation systems can be categorized into three main market types: rooftop, ground, and carport. Rooftop installations involve placing solar panels on the roofs of buildings, which is a common and popular choice for residential and commercial properties. Ground installations are placed on the ground and are often used for larger solar farms or in areas with limited roof space. Carport installations involve integrating solar panels into parking structures, providing both shade for vehicles and renewable energy generation.</p></p>
