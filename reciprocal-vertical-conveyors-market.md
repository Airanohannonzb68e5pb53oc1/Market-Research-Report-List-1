There are three main types of reciprocal vertical conveyors on the market: Z-Type, CType, and E-Type. Z-Type conveyors have a vertical lift with a horizontal extension at the top, ideal for transferring materials between two different elevations. CType conveyors have a compact design that allows for efficient use of space. E-Type conveyors are known for their energy efficiency and smooth operation, making them suitable for high-volume material handling applications.</p></p>
