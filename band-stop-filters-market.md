<p><p>Band stop filters are used to pass signals within a certain frequency range while attenuating signals outside of that range. There are several types of band stop filters including notch filters, cavity filters, tunable filters, and ceramic filters. Notch filters are designed to reject a specific frequency range, cavity filters use resonant cavities to achieve band rejection, tunable filters allow for adjustment of the stop band within a certain range, and ceramic filters are made of ceramic material for high frequencies and stability. Each type has its own unique applications and advantages in filtering unwanted signals.</p></p>
