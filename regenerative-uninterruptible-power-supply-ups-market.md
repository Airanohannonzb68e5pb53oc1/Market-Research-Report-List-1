<p><p>Regenerative UPS systems utilize a regenerative converter to convert energy from AC to DC during power outages. Sinusoidal PWM UPS systems use pulse-width modulation to create a sine wave output, ensuring clean and reliable power. Matrix converter UPS systems offer efficient power conversion with multiple input and output phases. Each type of UPS market caters to different power requirements and offers unique benefits such as energy regeneration, sine wave output, and high efficiency in power conversion.</p></p>
