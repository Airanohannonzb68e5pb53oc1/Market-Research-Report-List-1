<p><p>Microbial inoculants are classified into three main types: Bacterial, Fungal Inoculants, and Composite Inoculants. Bacterial inoculants are formulations containing beneficial bacteria that help improve soil health and plant growth. Fungal inoculants, on the other hand, contain beneficial fungi that promote plant nutrient uptake. Composite inoculants combine both bacteria and fungi in a single formulation, providing a comprehensive approach to enhancing soil fertility and plant health. Each type of inoculant offers unique benefits in promoting sustainable agriculture practices.</p></p>
