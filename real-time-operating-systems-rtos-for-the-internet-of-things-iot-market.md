<p><p>Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS) for the Internet of Things (IoT) market types include Hard Real-Time Operating Systems, Firm Real-Time Operating Systems, and Soft Real-Time Operating Systems. Hard RTOS guarantees that tasks are completed within a strict time frame, crucial for safety-critical applications. Firm RTOS ensures timely completion of tasks, providing some flexibility in timing requirements. Soft RTOS offers more flexibility, allowing tasks to be completed whenever possible without strict timing constraints. Each type caters to different IoT applications based on their timing requirements.</p></p>
