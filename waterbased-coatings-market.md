<p><p>Waterbased coatings market can be broadly categorized into various types based on the resin used. The most common resin types include acrylic (Styrene Acrylic), Formaldehyde, Polyurethane, Alkyds, Epoxy, and Fluoropolymer. Acrylic coatings offer good durability and weather resistance, while polyurethane coatings provide excellent chemical and abrasion resistance. Alkyds are popular for their fast drying properties, while epoxy coatings are known for their high strength and adhesion. Fluoropolymer coatings are valued for their exceptional non-stick and low friction properties.</p></p>
