<p><p>Waste-to-Energy (WTE) market includes various types, with incineration being the most common method. In this process, waste is burned at high temperatures to generate heat, which is then used to produce electricity. Other markets within the WTE sector include gasification, pyrolysis, and anaerobic digestion. Gasification involves converting waste into synthesis gas for electricity generation, pyrolysis uses high temperatures in the absence of oxygen to break down waste, and anaerobic digestion generates biogas through the decomposition of organic matter.</p></p>
