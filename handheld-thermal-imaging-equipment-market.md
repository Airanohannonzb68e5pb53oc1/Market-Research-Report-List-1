<p><p>Handheld thermal imaging equipment can be categorized into two main types: infrared thermal imaging and microwave thermal imaging. </p><p>Infrared thermal imaging technology detects infrared radiation emitted by objects and converts it into a visible image, making it ideal for applications such as night vision, building inspections, and security surveillance. </p><p>On the other hand, microwave thermal imaging uses microwave sensors to detect temperature differences, making it suitable for detecting hidden objects, detecting fires, and monitoring industrial processes. </p><p>Both types of thermal imaging equipment offer valuable insights in various industries.</p></p>
