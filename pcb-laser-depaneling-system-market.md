<p><p>PCB laser depaneling systems are categorized into three types: CO2 laser depaneling system, fiber laser depaneling system, and others. CO2 laser depaneling systems use a carbon dioxide laser source for precise cutting of PCBs. Fiber laser depaneling systems utilize a fiber laser source for high-speed and high-precision cutting. Each type of laser depaneling system offers unique advantages and capabilities for different applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
