<p><ul><li>LIFO Type</li><li>FIFO Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radio Shuttle System is a high-density storage solution used in warehouses and distribution centers. The LIFO type operates on a Last-In-First-Out basis, meaning the last pallet loaded is the first to be unloaded. This system is suitable for products with a long shelf life. On the other hand, the FIFO type operates on a First-In-First-Out basis, where the first pallet loaded is the first to be unloaded. This system is ideal for products with expiration dates or quick turnover. Both types serve different market needs based on inventory management strategies.</p></p>
