<p><strong>The Flocculant For Mining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inorganic Flocculant</li><li>Organic Flocculant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flocculants for mining can be classified into two types: inorganic flocculants and organic flocculants. Inorganic flocculants are typically metal salts like aluminum sulfate or iron chloride, which work by neutralizing the charge of particles in the slurry. On the other hand, organic flocculants are synthetic polymers like polyacrylamide, which form bridges between particles to create larger flocs. Both types are used in the mining industry to improve solid-liquid separation efficiency and clarify process water.</p></p>
