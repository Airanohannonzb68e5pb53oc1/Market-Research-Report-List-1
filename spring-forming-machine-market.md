<p><strong>The Spring Forming Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DualAxis</li><li>Triaxial</li><li>FourAxis</li><li>FiveAxis</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spring forming machines are categorized based on the number of axes they operate on. Dual-axis machines are capable of performing bending and cutting in two directions. Triaxial machines add a twisting function, while four-axis machines include the ability to rotate the wire during forming. Five-axis machines offer the highest level of precision and flexibility, allowing for complex spring shapes. Other types may include specialized machines with unique features to cater to specific forming requirements in the market. Each type offers different capabilities to meet various spring forming needs.</p></p>
