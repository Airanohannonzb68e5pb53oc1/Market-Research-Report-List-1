<p><p>Carpet adhesives are available in various types depending on the material and purpose of the carpet installation. Epoxy adhesives offer strong bonding for heavy-duty commercial use. Urethane adhesives provide flexibility and resistance to moisture. Acrylic adhesives are popular for their ease of use and quick drying time. Vinyl adhesives are known for their durability and resistance to high traffic areas. Other types of adhesives include pressure-sensitive and tackifiers, each offering unique benefits for specific carpet installation needs.</p></p>
