<p><p>Automated Manual Transmission (AMT) is a type of semi-automatic transmission system that combines the benefits of both manual and automatic transmissions. It features an automatic clutch and electronically controlled actuators to shift gears, making it easier and more convenient for drivers to operate compared to traditional manual transmissions. AMT systems offer improved fuel efficiency, reduced driver fatigue, and smoother gear shifts.</p><p>The Automated Manual Transmission (AMT) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period.
